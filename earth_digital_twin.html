Earth Digital Twin

A browser-based 3D globe with realistic satellite imagery, atmospheric lighting, night-view population lights, and live OpenStreetMap streets and buildings that stream in as you descend from orbit.
Search for a place or use a preset flight path.
Interactive layers
Street-level overlays are requested only when you zoom close to the ground, then cached locally in the session to keep the browser responsive. Night mode uses NASA Black Marble population-light imagery and will automatically use browser-safe tokens from the local server.
  • Scroll to zoom from orbit down to city scale.
  • Hold right mouse to tilt and orbit the camera.
  • Search places globally using OpenStreetMap geocoding.
